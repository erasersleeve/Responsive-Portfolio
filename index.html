01. About Me

I've always been interested in computer science, having built several of my own computers and doing some basic game modding over the years. But I never had much experience coding until the winter of 2019 when I had time off from working and began to teach myself Javascript. More of a curiosity project than anything I got the fundamentals down but took a break when I went back to work that spring. The seed had been planted though, and I soon was thinking of how to develop my skills further. I considered going back to school for a second degree, and even enrolled in classes but heard good things about the University of Pennsylvania's Coding Bootcamp. So, in 2020 I enrolled in that course and had a great --if challenging-- time. Since then I've been working for 2u education as a centralize grader for their coding programs and am looking to find a role as a developer.
